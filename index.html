About the Event

A 24-hour Hackathon for Mozilla's mobile operating system FirefoxOS. This event will help students to familiarize with all the aspects of Firefox OS, WebApps, WebAPIs. It will also be a great opportunity for students to create quality apps and submit to marketplace.

  • 24-hour Hackathon
  • Development of apps for devices running Firefox OS
  • Team members : 2-3

Things to Know

  • You are expected to have adequate knowledge in building webapps and / or enough experience with Web Technologies (HTML, CSS, JavaScript, WebAPI)
  • You have to produce your College ID cards at the venue.
  • Bring your own laptop (or device to hack on).
  • Registration Fee (Per Individual)
    • Without Meals : ₹100
    • With Meals : ₹200
    PS : Refreshment will be provided for all.
